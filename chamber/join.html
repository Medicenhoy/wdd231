Non-Profit Membership Benefits

Designed strictly for non-profit organizations and NGOs.

  • Cost: Free / No Fee
  • Basic directory listing.
  • Access to general chamber training workshops.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Perfect for small businesses and startups looking to expand networking.

  • Cost: $150 / year
  • Standard directory listing.
  • 10% discount on chamber events tickets.
  • Access to monthly networking mixers.

Silver Membership Benefits

Our mid-tier level with advanced branding capabilities.

  • Cost: $350 / year
  • Enhanced listing with company logo.
  • 25% discount on events and advertising slots.
  • Access to exclusive training seminars.

Gold Membership Benefits

Maximum exposure, elite status, and top-tier promotional placements.

  • Cost: $600 / year
  • Premium listing at the top of the directory searches.
  • Free admission to all chamber events (up to 3 members).
  • Home Page Spotlight placement eligibility!
